PEANUT BUTTER CUPS RECIPE

MOSE KWAK
difficulty level: EASY
calories/cup: 123
time required: 20 minutes
serving size: 3 dozens

INGREDIENTS NEEDED:

1 cup creamy peanut butter

2cups (12 ounces) chocolate chips

1/2 cup confectioners' sugar

4 milk chocolate candy bars (1.55 ounces each), coarsely chopped

4-1/2 teaspoons of butter(softened)

1/2 teaspoon salt

colored sprinkles(optional)

STEPS


1. Combine 1/2 cup peanut butter, confectioners' sugar, butter and salt intul smooth.

2. In a microwave, melt chocolate chips, candy bars and remaining peanut butter; stir until smooth

3. Drop teaspoonfuls of chocolate mixture into paper-lined miniature muffin cups. Drop a scant teaspoonful of peanut butter mixture into each cup; top with another teaspoonful of chocolate mixture.

If desired, decorate with sprinkles. Refrigerate until set. Store in an airtight container.
